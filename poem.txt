As the winter sun begins to lay down on the immature land, the saplings begin to emerge; spring rushes into the lands lending the earth youthful garments to wear. 
Thereupon, the eyes of the earth open after a year of sleeping, and the plants arise as if brides are adorned with every kind of jewelry.
The branches of trees intertwine and then seperate; and the fragrances reveal their secrets to the breeze.
